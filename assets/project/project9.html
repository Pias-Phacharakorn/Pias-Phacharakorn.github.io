Autodesk COBie

What?

  • Implemented COBie (Construction Operations Building Information Exchange) data delivery for projects.
  • Extracted and organized asset information from BIM models into COBie-compliant formats.
  • Ensured data quality and consistency for handover to facility management teams.
  • Aimed to provide structured, usable data for building operations and maintenance.

How?

  • Utilized Revit and other BIM software to populate COBie parameters.
  • Used specialized tools or scripts to export COBie data in spreadsheet format.
  • Performed data validation and cleansing to ensure accuracy.
  • Collaborated with project teams to define COBie requirements early in the project lifecycle.
