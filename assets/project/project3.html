Autodesk Construction Cloud Handbook

What?

  • Created a comprehensive handbook for Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC) for internal use.
  • Documented best practices for document management, model coordination, and project collaboration within ACC.
  • Provided step-by-step guides for common ACC workflows.
  • Aimed to standardize ACC usage across projects and improve team efficiency.

How?

  • Researched and compiled information from Autodesk documentation and internal project experiences.
  • Organized content logically with clear headings and examples.
  • Used screenshots and diagrams to illustrate complex procedures.
  • Conducted internal reviews to ensure accuracy and usability of the handbook.
