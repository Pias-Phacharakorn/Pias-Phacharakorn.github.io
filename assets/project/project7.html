PSM Donmueang

What?

  • Created a comprehensive BIM model for the PSM Donmueang project
  • Ensured accurate representation of all building components for coordination and analysis.
  • Aimed to facilitate interdisciplinary coordination and clash detection.
  • Provided a detailed digital twin for project planning and execution.

How?

  • Utilized Revit for detailed modeling of architectural, structural, and civil components.
  • Integrated design data from various disciplines into a federated model.
  • Performed regular clash detection using Navisworks to identify and resolve conflicts.
  • Generated various reports and quantities directly from the BIM model.
